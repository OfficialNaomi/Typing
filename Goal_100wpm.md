# Typing Speed Improvement Plan: The Road to 100 WPM

## The Core Philosophy: Accuracy over Raw Speed
At 70 WPM, basic muscle memory is established, but fine motor errors and hesitation are the primary bottlenecks. Hitting backspace destroys rhythm and flow. 
**Goal:** Reach a baseline of 100 WPM with strictly >98% accuracy.

## Phase 1: Foundation & "Proof of Work"
Documenting the authentic journey from the ground up to show the real grind behind the speed.
*   **KTouch:** Completed levels 1 and 2, currently working through level 3 to lock in fundamental finger placement.
*   **Typing Club:** Executing a full 100% completion run to build up to a solid ~60 WPM baseline. This serves as the documented "proof of work."

## Phase 2: Eradicating Weak Keys
The focus here is entirely on retraining muscle memory for specific problem keys without the cognitive load of reading actual sentences.
*   **Tool:** [Keybr.com](https://www.keybr.com/) or Linux native `gtypist`.
*   **Method:** Allow the algorithm to identify the weakest keys. Do not move on until the current set is consistently typed with 98%+ accuracy.

## Phase 3: N-gram Mastery & Contextual Flow
Moving from 80 WPM to 100+ requires typing in combinations (n-grams) and looking ahead in the text.
*   **Tool:** [Monkeytype](https://monkeytype.com/) (Quotes mode, punctuation and numbers ON).
*   **Method:** Keep your eyes one or two words ahead. If you make a mistake, use `Ctrl + Backspace` to delete the entire word and retype it. 

## Practice Mindset: Have Fun With It
No rigid daily routines, no strict timers. A forced daily schedule doesn't make sense—when you enjoy the process and remove the pressure, you will naturally want to type more. 
*   Play with the different sites whenever you feel like it.
*   Do what you want, when you want. The speed will come automatically as long as you are having fun and staying accurate.

## The "100 WPM" Milestones
- [ ] Complete KTouch Level 3.
- [ ] 100% Completion of the Typing Club curriculum.
- [ ] Achieve 98% consistent accuracy on Keybr across the entire alphabet.
- [ ] Hit 100 WPM peak on short bursts (Monkeytype 15-word tests).
- [ ] **Final Goal:** Reach 100 WPM on a full 60-second test with punctuation.
